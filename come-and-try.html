Come & Try

one pipeline · every club in Queensland · fully automated

One website for every club in Queensland. Someone searches for croquet, finds the site, picks a session near them, and lands in a shared pipeline that any committee member can see and follow up.

Small regional clubs get the same tools as large city ones. No warm leads go cold because the right person was on holiday.

How it works

  • Prospect enters postcode — gets matched to their nearest clubs
  • Sign-up triggers an automated 28-day nurture email sequence
  • Club committee gets visibility immediately — no manual forwarding
  • Pipeline tracks every prospect: new → contacted → booked → attended → joined
  • Works without any volunteer touching it until the personal follow-up moment
