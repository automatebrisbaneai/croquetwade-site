Ideals & Aims

4 ideals · 6 aims · developed from Management Committee workshop

The strategic framework that governs how CAQ operates and what it builds toward. The ideals are the decision filters — every action must pass them. The aims are the six outcomes the organisation works toward.

The 4 Ideals

IDEAL 1
Enjoy Croquet
IDEAL 2
Keep It Simple
IDEAL 3
Hit Our Aims
IDEAL 4
Co-operate for Croquet

The 6 Aims

  • 1 Strengthen Admin & Internal Operations
  • 2 Promote Croquet Externally
  • 3 Grow & Engage Members
  • 4 Support Clubs
  • 5 Develop Skilled Play
  • 6 Encourage Innovative Ideas

The aims form a cascade — strong admin enables external promotion, which brings people to the door, which feeds member growth, which strengthens clubs, which develops play, which opens room for innovation.
